Public Employment Service Office (PESO)

The Public Employment Service Office (PESO) serves as a non-fee charging multi-employment service facility dedicated to achieving full employment and equal employment opportunities for all. It operates on a community-based model and is primarily supported and maintained by local government units, non-governmental organizations, community-based organizations, and state universities and colleges.

The Public Employment Service Office (PESO) in Camarines Norte serves as a crucial link between job seekers and employers. It provides services to help people in Camarines Norte find jobs, offers career guidance, and builds partnerships with businesses.

Find Jobs

The BaLink Trabaho website, in collaboration with PESO dedicated government office committed to enhancing employment opportunities, proudly presents a comprehensive platform showcasing a myriad of job offerings. This online portal serves as a valuable resource, connecting job seekers with prospective employers and contributing to the economic development of the region. Explore the PESO website to discover a range of employment opportunities tailored to the vibrant community of Camarines Norte, as we strive to foster growth, prosperity, and career advancement for all.
